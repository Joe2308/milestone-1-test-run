James

Meet James, the main man and front man of Picture Perfect Wedding Band. In addition to unmatched range James has great variety in his vocal timbre. From falsetto to deep bass he is a talented vocalist, as a result whatever additions we make to our set we can perform them with style and professionalism. As well as vocal talent James has the stage presence and charisma to work any crowd. Before he sings a single lyric he will have the dance floor full and the room full of electric energy in anticipation of a live performance to be remembered. This is what makes us one of the best wedding bands in Ireland.

Conan

Meet Conan. Conan plays drums and also sings backing vocals and harmonies. Conan has many years experience in the wedding entertainment industry. In addition to having played in some of the best wedding bands he has also had many years of success with well known Irish tribute bands. Because he has over 15 years of live gigging under his belt Conan has the tools to keep the band grooving and your feet stomping. Therefore he is the first ingredient in hypnotising your guests on to the dance floor.

J Johnson

Because of his exstensive background in blues bands he brings a rocky edge to Picture Perfect with his tasty guitar licks and disctinctive vocal growl. With his plethora of talents Johnny makes the band stand out from the rest. Whether the song calls for soft finesse or bluesy grit Johnnyâ€™s got ya covered!.

Derek

Meet Derek. Derek plays bass guitar and sings harmony. Derek plays both five and four string bass guitars so he knows how to bring the low end! He has over 20 years of experience playing in wedding bands and has played in many well known bands to hone his skills on the bass. Derek holds down the low end in style with his tight grooves and walks he sets the tone with Conan to get the crowd bouncing.

Johnny

Meet Johnny. Johnny plays many instruments, his staples are lead guitar and keyboards but also plays fiddle and mandolin. With an envious and vast collection of instruments, Johnny could open his own guitar shop in the morning but he chooses instead to bring all his equipment to Picture Perfect gigs!. Johnny has many years of experience performing in some of the best wedding bands on the scene. He started as a young lad in an AC DC tribute band. Because Johnny is a one man band there are no limits to the genres Picture Perfect can do justice to and most importantly there are no backing tracks. What you see is what you hear.
