About US

Meet the Band

what you get when you book us

Recoil are a talented five piece based in Ireland and tour both Ireland and the UK regularly. As well as many happy couples we have long and established relationships with many large companies and are their entertainment of choice year after year.

We are a self managed and experienced act. We know what it takes to pull off the spectacular. All the guys in the band are fun and outgoing and this really shows when we are on stage together.

Recoil have weekly showcases and we invite you join us the next time we are in your location. Our gig calender is always up to date on our Facebook page. We would love if you became a follower!

meet the band

chris

Meet Chris, the front man and lead vocalist for Recoil. Aswell as unmatched vocal range Chris has great rapport with any audience. His charisma and stage presence allow for an entertaining night no matter how big or small the event may be.

Conan

Meet Conan. Conan is a talented drummer and is a vital ingredient in getting those toes tapping and hands clapping. His steady groove will have you on the dancefloor quicker than you can say "that's my jam!".

joe

Meet Joe. Joe has over 15 years experience as a lead guitarist and his ability allows Recoil to take on many different genres from soothing fingerstyle acoustic to blistering rock solos. Don't forget your air guitar!

nathan

Meet Nathan. Nathan plays bass guitar and also has an amazing vocal. Nathan holds down the low end in style. His funky basslines combined with Conan's groove ensures a full dancefloor night after night.

Johnny

Meet Johnny. Johnny plays many instruments. His staples are lead guitar and keyboards but also plays fiddle and mandolin. Johnny is a supreme musician and allows Recoil to take their show to the next level.
